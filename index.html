Experiences

I have over 10 years of experience as a paralegal but looking to take my organizational and management skills to a new arena.

The world is constantly looking to the web to answer its questions and I want to help people find those answers. Designing and coding websites has always been something I've found fascinating. In fact back in 1999, I was enrolled in a web design program at UC Irvine. Unfortunately I was not able to finish the program yet I have taken those programming and design skills with me everywhere. In fact, at one point I coded one of the pages for the FOIA section of Bonneville Power Administration's website even though I was hired as a paralegal to assist with their e-discovery platform and backlog of FOIA requests.

I love learning new things, especially new technologies and am looking for opportunities where I can utilize the things I've learned in my program at SU and while continuing to learn and expand that knowledge in my career.
